WHAT IS THIS SYSTEM?

Your Mail Management System helps you:
- Track all incoming mail (packages and letters) for your customers
- Send automatic notifications when mail arrives
- Calculate storage fees for unclaimed mail
- Keep organized records of pickups and payments
- See business statistics and reports

Think of it like a digital logbook that replaces pen-and-paper tracking, with added benefits like automatic customer emails and fee calculations.

WHAT IF I MAKE A MISTAKE?

Don't panic! Most actions can be fixed:

Logged wrong customer?
- Click "Edit" on the mail item
- Change customer in dropdown
- Save

Marked as picked up by accident?
- Click "Edit" on the item
- Change status back to "Awaiting Pickup"
- Save

Sent notification to wrong person?
- Can't unsend (already emailed)
- Send correction email if needed
- Double-check customer before clicking next time

Recorded wrong payment amount?
- Click "Edit" on the payment
- Update amount
- Save

Deleted something by accident?
- Contact your manager
- May need developer help to restore
- This is why we have backups!

TUTORIAL 3: HOW TO SCAN AND LOG NEW MAIL (USING CAMERA)
=============================================================================

When to use this: When new mail arrives and you want to quickly log it using your phone or computer camera

Step 1: Click "Intake" in the left menu

Step 2: Click "Scan Mail" button

Step 3: Allow camera access
- Your browser will ask "Allow camera access?" 
- Click "Allow" or "Yes"

Step 4: Point your camera at the mail label
- Hold steady
- Make sure the label is clearly visible
- Good lighting helps!

Step 5: Click "Capture" or "Take Photo"

Step 6: Wait for AI to process (3-5 seconds)
- The system will try to:
  * Read the recipient name
  * Find matching customer
  * Read tracking number (if visible)

Step 7: Review the auto-filled information
- Customer Name: Check if it matched correctly
- Type: Select "Package" or "Letter"
- Tracking Number: Add if not detected
- Notes: Add any special details

Step 8: If customer name is WRONG:
- Click the dropdown
- Select the correct customer manually
- Or click "Add New Contact" if they're not in the system

Step 9: Click "Submit" to save

Step 10: Success! The mail is now logged in the system

TIPS:
- If the camera won't open, try a different browser (Chrome works best)
- Make sure you have good lighting - hold the mail near a window or lamp
- If AI can't read the label, you can always enter information manually
- You can take multiple photos if the first one isn't clear

TUTORIAL 4: HOW TO MANUALLY LOG NEW MAIL (WITHOUT SCANNING)
=============================================================================

When to use this: When you don't have a camera available, or prefer typing

Step 1: Click "Intake" in the left menu

Step 2: Click "Manual Entry" button (or skip the scan step)

Step 3: Fill in the form:

REQUIRED FIELDS:
- Customer: Click dropdown and select customer name
  * If customer is new, click "Add New Contact" first
- Type: Select "Package" or "Letter"
- Received Date: Usually today's date (auto-filled)

OPTIONAL BUT RECOMMENDED:
- Tracking Number: USPS, UPS, FedEx tracking number
- Carrier: USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, etc.
- Weight: Package weight (letters can be left blank)
- Dimensions: Length x Width x Height
- Notes: "Fragile", "Perishable", "Hold for pickup", etc.

Step 4: Click "Submit" or "Log Mail"

Step 5: Success! The mail is now in the system

WHEN TO USE MANUAL ENTRY:
- Camera isn't working
- Label is damaged or unreadable
- You're logging multiple items quickly
- You prefer typing over scanning

TUTORIAL 6: HOW TO MARK MAIL AS PICKED UP
=============================================================================

When to use this: When a customer picks up their mail

Step 1: Click "Mail" in the left menu

Step 2: Find the mail item that was picked up

- If someone else picks up, note their name in "Picked up by" field

TUTORIAL 7: HOW TO CALCULATE AND RECORD STORAGE FEES
=============================================================================

When to use this: When mail has been stored for 30+ days and fees are owed

HOW STORAGE FEES WORK:
- First 30 days: FREE
- After 30 days: $2/day for packages, $1/day for letters
- Fees calculate automatically

---

METHOD 1: VIEW FEES FROM DASHBOARD

Step 1: Go to Dashboard (click "Dashboard" in left menu)

Step 2: Look at the "Outstanding Storage Fees" section
- Shows total fees owed by all customers
- Click to see breakdown by customer

---

METHOD 2: VIEW FEES FOR SPECIFIC CUSTOMER

Step 1: Click "Contacts" in left menu

Step 2: Click on customer's name

Step 3: Scroll to "Outstanding Fees" section
- Shows all unpaid storage fees
- Lists which mail items have fees

---

METHOD 3: RECORD FEE PAYMENT

When customer pays their storage fees:

Step 1: Click "Contacts" in left menu

Step 2: Click on customer's name

Step 3: Find the "Outstanding Fees" section

Step 4: Click "Record Payment" next to the fee

Step 5: Enter payment details:
- Amount Paid: How much they paid
- Payment Method: Cash, Check, Credit Card, etc.
- Payment Date: Usually today
- Notes: Check number, receipt number, etc.

Step 6: Click "Save Payment"

Step 7: Success! Fee is marked as paid

WHAT HAPPENS:
- Fee status changes from "Unpaid" to "Paid"
- Total outstanding balance updates
- Payment is recorded in customer history
- You can print a receipt if needed

TIPS:
- Always give customer a receipt when they pay
- Record partial payments if they can't pay full amount
- Check fees weekly to follow up with customers
- You can waive fees in special cases (talk to manager first)

TUTORIAL 12: HOW TO UPDATE YOUR PASSWORD
=============================================================================

When to use this: To change your login password for security

METHOD 1: CHANGE PASSWORD WHILE LOGGED IN

Step 1: Log into the app

Step 2: Click your name or profile icon (top right corner)

Step 3: Click "Settings"

Step 4: Find "Change Password" section

Step 5: Fill in:
- Current Password: Your old password
- New Password: Your new password (make it strong!)
- Confirm New Password: Type new password again

Step 6: Click "Update Password"

Step 7: Success! Use your new password next time you log in

---

METHOD 2: RESET PASSWORD (IF YOU FORGOT IT)

Step 1: Go to login page

Step 2: Click "Forgot Password?" link

Step 3: Enter your email address

Step 4: Click "Send Reset Link"

Step 5: Check your email inbox
- Look for email from "Mei Way Mail Plus" or "Supabase"
- Check spam folder if you don't see it

Step 6: Click the link in the email

Step 7: Enter your new password (twice)

Step 8: Click "Reset Password"

Step 9: Success! You can now log in with your new password

---

PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS:
- At least 8 characters long
- Should include:
  * Uppercase letters (A-Z)
  * Lowercase letters (a-z)
  * Numbers (0-9)
  * Special characters (!@#$%^&*)

GOOD PASSWORD EXAMPLES:
- MailForward2024!
- MySecurePass#99
- BlueOcean$2026

BAD PASSWORD EXAMPLES (don't use these):
- password
- 123456
- myname
- qwerty

TIPS:
- Change your password every 3-6 months
- Don't share your password with anyone
- Don't write it down where others can see it
- Use a password manager if you have trouble remembering

TUTORIAL 13: TROUBLESHOOTING COMMON ISSUES
=============================================================================

ISSUE 1: CAN'T LOG IN

PROBLEM: "Invalid credentials" error
SOLUTION:
1. Check your email is spelled correctly
2. Check Caps Lock is OFF
3. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Contact admin if still can't log in

---

ISSUE 2: CAMERA WON'T OPEN FOR SCANNING

PROBLEM: Black screen or "Camera access denied"
SOLUTION:
1. Click "Allow" when browser asks for camera permission
2. Check your computer/phone camera isn't being used by another app
3. Try a different browser (Chrome works best)
4. On iPhone: Settings ‚Üí Safari ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Allow
5. On Android: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Browser ‚Üí Permissions ‚Üí Camera
6. If still doesn't work, use Manual Entry instead

---

ISSUE 3: CUSTOMER SAYS THEY DIDN'T GET EMAIL NOTIFICATION

PROBLEM: Notification was sent but customer didn't receive it
SOLUTION:
1. Go to customer's profile
2. Check "Notifications" tab to see if it was sent
3. Verify customer's email address is correct
4. Ask customer to check spam/junk folder
5. Resend the notification
6. Update email address if it's wrong

---

ISSUE 4: CAN'T FIND A MAIL ITEM

PROBLEM: Mail is logged but can't find it in the list
SOLUTION:
1. Check search spelling (use tracking number)
2. Clear all filters (click "Clear Filters")
3. Change date range to "All Time"
4. Check if it was marked as "Picked Up" by mistake
5. Search by customer name instead of tracking number

---

ISSUE 5: STORAGE FEES CALCULATING WRONG

PROBLEM: Fee amount doesn't look right
SOLUTION:
1. Check the "Received Date" is correct
2. Fees start after 30 days
3. Rate: $2/day for packages, $1/day for letters
4. Math: (Days waiting - 30) √ó Daily rate
5. If still wrong, contact technical support

---

ISSUE 6: PAGE WON'T LOAD / "ERROR" MESSAGE

PROBLEM: White screen or error message
SOLUTION:
1. Refresh the page (press F5 or Cmd+R)
2. Clear your browser cache:
   - Chrome: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data
   - Safari: Settings ‚Üí Clear History
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. Try again in 5-10 minutes (server might be updating)
6. Contact support if issue persists

---

ISSUE 7: SLOW PERFORMANCE / PAGE LOADING FOREVER

PROBLEM: App is very slow or stuck loading
SOLUTION:
1. Check your internet speed (run a speed test)
2. Close other browser tabs
3. Restart your browser
4. Clear browser cache
5. The free server "sleeps" after 15 minutes - first load takes 30-60 seconds
6. After that, it should be fast

---

ISSUE 8: CAN'T SEND NOTIFICATIONS / EMAILS FAILING

PROBLEM: "Failed to send notification" error
SOLUTION:
1. Check you're connected to the internet
2. Verify customer email address is valid
3. Check if Gmail has hit 500 emails/day limit
4. Wait 5 minutes and try again
5. Contact technical support if problem continues

---

ISSUE 9: CUSTOMER INFO WON'T UPDATE

PROBLEM: Changes to customer profile won't save
SOLUTION:
1. Fill in all required fields (marked with *)
2. Check email format is correct (must have @)
3. Click "Save" button
4. Refresh page to see if changes saved
5. Try editing again

---

ISSUE 10: DASHBOARD NUMBERS LOOK WRONG

PROBLEM: Statistics don't match reality
SOLUTION:
1. Refresh the page (press F5)
2. Check date filter - make sure it's set correctly
3. Dashboard updates every hour - might be delayed
4. Count manually and compare
5. Report to technical support if numbers are way off
